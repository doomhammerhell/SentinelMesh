# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect all participation in SentinelMesh to be professional, respectful and technically constructive.

Examples of acceptable behavior:

- discussing technical disagreement with evidence
- giving actionable review feedback
- helping contributors improve proposals
- reporting security concerns responsibly

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, intimidation or personal attacks
- doxxing or publication of private information
- hostile or bad-faith review behavior
- discriminatory language or exclusionary conduct

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions or ban participants who violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Report incidents through the private security contact described in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
